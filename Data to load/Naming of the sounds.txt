

**The sounds loaded onto the SD card for service functions and alarm execution should be formatted as follows:**

1. Only **.WAV** file format is acceptable.
2. The bitrate can vary, but must not exceed **44100 Hz**. Mono or stereo formats are both supported.
3. File naming and their purposes are described below.

---

**Alarm processing**
- `alarm_default.wav` – default alarm sound. It will be played if no other alarm file is found.

**Bluetooth service**
- `sfx_ble_on.wav` – Bluetooth ON
- `sfx_ble_off.wav` – Bluetooth OFF
- `sfx_ble_connected.wav` – Bluetooth connected
- `sfx_ble_disconnected.wav` – Bluetooth disconnected

**System**
- `sfx_ok.wav` – confirmation of an action, value, or command
- `sfx_error.wav` – indicates an error during operation, an invalid value, an unknown command, etc.

**Additional functions**
- `chime_hourly_bell.wav` – played at the start of each hour (except during silent hours)
- `chime_quarter.wav` – played at each quarter of an hour (except during silent hours)
- `startup_greeting.wav` – startup sound, played when power is applied to the device
